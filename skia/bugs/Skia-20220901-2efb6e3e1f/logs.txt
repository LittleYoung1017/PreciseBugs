    Fix fuzzer-discovered error with intrinsic range checks.
    
    Intrinsic folding previously checked for out-of-range values by
    comparing against [-FLT_MAX, +FLT_MAX]. Now, we check against
    [returnType.minimumValue(), returnType.maximumValue()]. For floats,
    this gives the same behavior as before. For integer types, this sets
    a much tighter bound.
